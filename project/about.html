About Nigeria

Our History

Nigeria, located in West Africa, has a history dating back thousands of years. From ancient civilizations like the Nok culture (1500 BC - 500 AD) to powerful kingdoms such as the Benin Empire, Oyo Empire, and the Kanem-Bornu Empire, Nigeria's past is rich with remarkable achievements in art, governance, and trade.

The country gained independence from British colonial rule on October 1, 1960, and has since grown into Africa's largest economy and most populous nation.
