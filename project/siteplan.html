Discover Nigeria - Website Plan

Cultural Tourism and Information Website

Site Name

Discover Nigeria

This name clearly communicates the purpose of the website - to help visitors discover and explore Nigeria's rich culture, food, and travel destinations.

Optional domain: discover-nigeria.org

Site Purpose

The Discover Nigeria website serves as a comprehensive digital guide to Nigerian culture, cuisine, and tourism.

  • Showcase Nigeria's diverse cultural heritage and traditions
  • Provide information about popular Nigerian dishes and recipes
  • Highlight must-visit travel destinations and attractions
  • Offer practical travel information and tips for visitors
  • Promote cultural understanding and tourism in Nigeria

Scenarios

Target audience questions that will drive website content:

  • "I'm planning my first trip to Nigeria. What are the must-visit places and what should I know about local customs?"
  • "I want to experience authentic Nigerian cuisine. What are the most popular dishes and where can I find recipes to try cooking them?"
  • "What cultural festivals and events happen throughout the year in different regions of Nigeria?"
